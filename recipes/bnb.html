Beef and Broccoli

Description

This is a chinese dish called Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Hot water
  • Low sodium soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Sesame Oil
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook broccoli (4-5 minutes)
  2. Remove broccoli and cook beef (~2 min per side)
  3. Reduce heat to low and add sauce (simmer for 3 minutes)
  4. Add broccoli and stir
