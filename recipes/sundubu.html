Sundubu

Description

This is a korean dish called Sundubu

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Mushroom
  • Onion
  • Spring Onion
  • Minced Pork
  • Gochugaru
  • Kosher Salt
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water and Sundubu Paste in a pot
  2. Once water starts to boil, add sliced mushroom in and stir
  3. Add tofu and use spoon to gently break the tofu into smaller pieces
  4. Crack an egg on top of the stew
  5. Garnish with spring onion
